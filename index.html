Who We Are

Keeping Rossland swimming

The Rossland Heritage Pool Society is a community-led organization dedicated to preserving and revitalizing Rossland's beloved public pool. We believe every community deserves access to safe, clean, and welcoming aquatic facilities.


Through fundraising, events, and merchandise, we're working to ensure future generations can splash, swim, and thrive right here in Rossland.
